## Getting Started

### Initial Setup

1. **Download and Install**
   - Download the app from your device's app store or use Expo Go
   - Open the app and follow the on-screen prompts

2. **Sign In with Microsoft**
   - Tap "Sign In" on the welcome screen
   - Enter your Microsoft account credentials
   - Grant necessary permissions (notifications, background tasks)

3. **Enable Notifications**
   - Allow notifications when prompted
   - This ensures you receive alerts about new opportunities and milestones

4. **Configure Preferences**
   - Go to Profile → Settings
   - Adjust notification preferences
   - Enable background sync for automatic updates

   - Aim for 150 searches per day minimum
   - This unlocks bonus points

   - Spread searches throughout the day

## Automation Setup

### Step-by-Step Automation Guide

**Step 1: Enable PC Search**
1. Go to Automation screen
2. Find "PC Search" toggle
3. Toggle ON
4. Set frequency to "Every 30 minutes"
5. Set active hours (e.g., 9 AM - 5 PM)
6. Tap "Save"

**Step 2: Enable Mobile Search**
1. Toggle "Mobile Search" ON
2. Set frequency to "Every 45 minutes"
3. Set active hours (e.g., 6 PM - 11 PM)
4. Tap "Save"

**Step 3: Enable Quiz Automation**
1. Toggle "Daily Quizzes" ON
2. Set to "Auto-complete if possible"
3. Set time to "6 AM daily"
4. Tap "Save"

**Step 4: Monitor and Adjust**
1. Check account health daily
2. If health drops, disable one automation
3. Wait 2-3 days before re-enabling
4. Adjust frequencies based on results

### Automation Best Practices

**Do:**
- Start with one automation feature
- Monitor account health closely
- Adjust based on account warnings
- Vary search patterns
- Take breaks between searches

**Don't:**
- Enable all automations at once
- Run automations 24/7
- Use exact same searches repeatedly
- Ignore account health warnings
- Disable background sync

## Troubleshooting

**Issue: App Won't Start**
- Solution: Clear app cache and restart

- Check internet connection

**Issue: Notifications Not Working**
- Solution: Check notification settings
- Enable in Settings → Notifications
- Restart the app
- Reinstall if necessary

**Issue: Sync Not Working**
- Solution: Check internet connection
- Verify Microsoft account is connected
- Enable background app refresh
- Restart the app

**Issue: Low Earnings**
- Solution: Check account health
- Verify automation is enabled
- Review Analytics for patterns
- Increase activity gradually

**Issue: Account Health Warning**
- Solution: Disable one automation
- Reduce search frequency
- Take a 24-hour break
- Contact support if persists

## FAQ

**Q: Is this app safe to use?**
A: Yes, the app uses official Microsoft OAuth and follows all Bing Rewards terms. However, use responsibly and monitor your account health.

**Q: Will my account get banned?**
A: Not if you use the app responsibly. The app includes account health monitoring and safety features. Monitor warnings and adjust as needed.

**Q: How much can I earn?**
A: Earnings vary by activity. Typical users earn 50-150 points daily with automation, or 100-300 with active participation.

**Q: Can I use this on multiple devices?**
A: Yes, sign in with your Microsoft account on multiple devices. Your data syncs automatically.

**Q: How often does data sync?**
A: Background sync runs every hour by default. You can manually sync anytime from the home screen.

**Q: What if I disable automation?**
A: Your data is preserved. You can re-enable anytime. Your account health will improve after a break.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, go to Profile → Export Data to download your activity history.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes, all data is encrypted and never shared. You can delete your data anytime.

**Q: How do I redeem points?**
A: Use the Bing Rewards website directly. The app tracks points but redemption happens through Microsoft's official site.
