## Slide 7: Anti-Detection Strategy - Overview

**Six Layers of Protection**

The tool implements multiple anti-detection measures to minimize account ban risk:

1. Variable Search Intervals
2. User-Agent Rotation
3. Localized Trending Searches
4. Human-Like Scrolling
5. Random Request Delays
6. Optional Cooldown Periods

---

## Slide 8: Anti-Detection #1: Variable Intervals

**Randomized Search Spacing**

**Without Protection:**
- Search at 11:00, 11:05, 11:10 (fixed 5-min intervals)
- Pattern looks automated
- High detection risk

**With Protection:**
- Search at 11:00, 11:18, 11:31 (randomized 15-32 sec)
- Pattern looks human
- Lower detection risk

**Default:** 15-32 seconds between searches

---

## Slide 9: Anti-Detection #2: User-Agent Rotation

**Browser Identification Spoofing**

**Problem:** All searches from identical browser signature = bot detection

**Solution:** Rotate between multiple user-agent strings

**Example:**
- Search 1: Windows Chrome 126
- Search 2: macOS Chrome 126
- Search 3: Windows Edge 126

**Result:** Appears to come from different devices

---

## Slide 10: Anti-Detection #3: Localized Searches

**Location-Based Keywords**

**How It Works:**
1. Detect your IP address location
2. Generate location-relevant searches
3. Searches appear natural and local

**Example (New York IP):**
- "weather in New York"
- "best restaurants in NYC"
- "New York events today"

**Result:** Searches look like real user queries

---

## Slide 11: Anti-Detection #4-6: Additional Measures

**Three More Layers of Protection**

**Human-Like Scrolling**
- Simulates scrolling through search results
- Real users scroll; bots don't

**Random Request Delays**
- 500-2000ms delays between internal requests
- Prevents rapid-fire bot-like behavior

**Optional 15-Minute Cooldowns**
- Pause after every 3 searches
- Creates realistic usage patterns
- Extra safety for cautious users
