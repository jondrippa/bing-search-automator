Long-Term Safety Strategy

Frequency
Once Per Day
Use the tool no more than once per day to keep usage natural and low-risk.
Mix Behaviors
Manual + Automatic
Combine automatic and manual searches so activity looks human.
Randomize Times
Variable Schedule
Run activities at different times each day rather than a fixed time.
Monitor Points
Check Weekly
Monitor points weekly; investigate any sudden stops promptly.
Keep Backups
Secondary Account
Keep a secondary Microsoft account in case the primary is suspended.
Stay Informed
Regular Review
Regularly review your account for warnings or unusual activity.
Key Principle: Sustainable usage balances automation with realistic human behavior. The closer activity mimics real users, the lower the detection risk.
