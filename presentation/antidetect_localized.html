Anti-Detection: Localized Searches

1
How It Works
Detect your IP address location
Generate location-relevant searches
Searches appear natural and local
2
Example (New York IP)
Generated Searches
"weather in New York"
"best restaurants in NYC"
"New York events today"
3
Result
Searches look like real user queries instead of generic automated searches. Natural, location-specific searches are far less likely to trigger bot-detection algorithms.
