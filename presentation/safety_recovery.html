Account Restriction Recovery

1
Stop Immediately
Uninstall the tool
Don't use it again
2
Contact Support
Go to https://support.microsoft.com
Explain that you used an automated tool
3
Request Review
Ask Microsoft to review your account
Be honest about the violation
4
Wait for Response
Microsoft typically responds in 1–2 weeks
Check email for updates
⚠ Important Note
If your account is reinstated, use the tool more conservatively or not at all. Microsoft actively monitors for automation, and repeated violations may result in permanent suspension.
