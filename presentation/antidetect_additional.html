Human-Like Scrolling
Simulates scrolling through search results pages. Real users scroll through results; bots don't. This simulation adds authenticity to your search behavior.

Random Request Delays
Adds 500–2000ms random delays between internal requests. Prevents rapid-fire requests that look like bot activity and mimics natural human browsing speed.
Result: Prevents bot-like rapid-fire behavior
6
Optional 15-Minute Cooldowns
Pauses for 15 minutes after every 3 searches (if enabled). Provides extra safety by creating realistic usage patterns that mimic genuine user behavior with natural breaks.
Result: Creates realistic usage patterns
